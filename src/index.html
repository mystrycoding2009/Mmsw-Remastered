FROM PHYSICAL TO ONLINE EDUCATION

Google Classroom

manav mangal Group of Schools have registered with GOOGLE for GOOGLE CLASSROOM. It is one of the finest online platforms and is secure and user-friendly. To address beforehand the security concerns during online classes, we have created individual email IDs with GOOGLE for our students and teachers. Google Classroom does not only give us a platform for online classes but is serving as a great medium for Online assessment as well, its success rate being proved by the successful conduct of Unit test and Mid-term examination, both online.

Edusecure App Manav Mangal

manav mangal has launched the School Mobile App for the convenience of parents. This Mobile App helps parents have online access to Homework, Attendance, Circulars, School Messages, Announcements, Online Fee Payment, Test / Exam Results and Picture Gallery. The parents have round the clock access to all the above.
