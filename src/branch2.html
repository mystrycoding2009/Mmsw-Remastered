manav mangal School

This SMART LEARNER'S WORLD is located in Sector 11, Panchkula. The school enrols children of 3+ in to Nursery. The school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), Delhi and prepares students for the Board’s All India Secondary School Examination.

Google Classroom

The school has registered with GOOGLE for GOOGLE CLASSROOM. It is one of the finest online platforms and is secure and user-friendly. To address beforehand the security concerns during online classes, we have created individual email IDs with GOOGLE for our students and teachers. Google Classroom does not only give us a platform for online classes but is serving as a great medium for Online assessment as well, its success rate being proved by the successful conduct of Unit test and Mid-term examination, both online.

School Mobile App (Edusecure)

manav mangal has launched the School Mobile App for the convenience of the parents. This Mobile App helps parents have online access to Homework, Attendance, Circulars, School Messages, Announcements, Online Fee Payment, Test / Exam Results and Picture Gallery. The parents have round the clock access to all the above.

SCHOOL LIFE

Activities & Competitions

manav mangal offers a rich plethora of activities in fields like IT, art and craft, dance, yoga & music. A blend of this along with activities like debating, quizzing, poetry recitation, spellathon etc. creates the desired balance and an interesting mix of extra and co-curricular. Students participate in number of Inter-School competitions among Tricity schools and win laurels. Regular participation in group activities helps our students to imbibe the skills of effective communication, team work and inter personal skills.

Learn More

Sports

Besides intellectual, mental and emotional abilities, the holistic growth aims at the development of physical abilities too. Keeping this in mind, the school encompasses various sports like table tennis, volley ball, throwball, etc. A Sports Fest is organized every year during winter. Marchpast and a series of well-organised competitions in various games are conducted. The winners are awarded medals and Trophies.

Learn More

Performing Arts & Visual Arts

The school provides a platform to exhibit hidden skills and potential and allows to enhance creative passions. Performing arts include dance, music and oration. Most awaited is the Annual Cultural Fiesta in which there is mass participation of students wherein the students get a chance to develop and exhibit their skills on stage in the form of dances, skits, compering, multimedia creation, etc.

Learn More

Week Celebrations

As an initiative to create awareness about today’s sociological trends, we have Week Celebrations, e.g. Save the Earth/ Language/ Joy of Giving/ Traffic Awareness Week etc. and throughout the week we have activities/competitions, Circle Time for students and Teacher’s talk on that theme. On the culmination of these Celebrations, students are awarded Certificates of Merit, shown videos and presentations to reinforce the values imbibed during the week.

Trips

At manav mangal, we adopt a teaching methodology that is suited to the individual needs of learners and lesson plans are also based on multiple intelligence theories. We make sure that students are not taught in water-tight compartments and the focus is to impart experiential and application-based learning through and field trips to places like Museums, Historical Monuments, Botanical Gardens. Nature walks are an important part of the curriculum. Educational Trip cum Excursion is an annual feature which students look forward to whole year.

Learn More

Student Council & Houses

The school aims at teaching leadership skills and experiencing teamwork. Students are divided into four houses. Discipline, cleanliness and creating informative display boards are some of the duties designated to a house week-wise.
